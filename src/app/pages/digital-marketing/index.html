Choosing the Right SEO Partner

Big or small, businesses that win at search reap enormous benefits. But although SEO is technical, it’s not an exact science, and it takes time to show results (anyone who tells you otherwise is lying).

As a result, you're putting a lot of trust in a Search Engine Optimization partner. Here’s what you should be looking for from your search agency:

We are focused on people. A good SEO agency naturally lives and breathes the technical stuff, but ultimately people, not bots, do business with you. Therefore, your search marketing team should care about the customer experience.

Focused on people. SEO changes fast. There are new challenges all the time, from algorithm changes to the impact of voice. An agency that hasn't changed its approach in 3 years is hopelessly out of touch. But, on the other hand, a great SEO agency is constantly evolving.

Clear communication. Google's algorithm is complex. As a result, SEO decisions often involve weighing pros and cons. So it's essential that your SEO partner can clearly and transparently explain their recommendations.
